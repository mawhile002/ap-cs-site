Basic Elementrary

In this game coded in Scratch, follow a purple square on a journey through school as it learns all of the basics. Help it find lost objects and complete school. Use the arrow keys to manuver through the school. Try exiting school or visiting the teachers at different scores, they may have something else to say!
I made this game as the final project for my AP Computer Science Principles class in the year 2019. I based the game off of the 'find object' game that the program Google CS teaches its students to make. I added new graphics, depth, and a moving animation for my square.
